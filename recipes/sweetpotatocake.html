Sweet Potato Cake

Description

This one is indeed a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs sweet potatoes
  • 1/3 cup diced green onions
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup plain bread crumbs
  • 1 Tbsp minced fresh cilantro
  • 2 Tbsp sour cream
  • 1 large clove of garlic
  • Salt and fresh cracked pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Mash sweet potatoes in a mixing bowl until smooth..
  2. Add green onions, cilantro, sour cream, egg, bread crumbs, grated garlic and mix well.
  3. Add salt and pepper, mix well and taste to make sure you have enough.
  4. Preheat a medium saute pan over medium heat. Add some vegetable oil.
  5. Form sweet potato patties and cook for about 5-7 minutes on each side. Use a small spatula and flip carefully.
